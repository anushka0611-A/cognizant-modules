Community Bulletin

The city council announces new infrastructure projects beginning next month. Residents are encouraged to attend the town hall meeting to share their feedback. Roads in the south district will undergo repair work for two weeks. Water supply may be interrupted on alternate days during this period. New bus routes will be added to improve connectivity across all wards. The library will extend its working hours during summer holidays. Citizens can register complaints online through the city portal. A new park will be inaugurated near the central market next Sunday.
